# Brainsimulation 3.0 – Introducing simple, fast, and reproducible workflows with TVB-O & TVB-Optim

showcase our new framework for specifying, running, and optimizing simulations

> **Goal**: Our team learns how to use the tools and is convinced of their ease of use.

## Structure (1h + Discussion)

1. Introduction into the Framework. Which problems do we want to solve? (5min)
   tvbo <--> tvboptim
2. tvbo pitch (15min)
3. tvboptim pitch (15min)
4. Hands-On: Exploration (30)
   - One Network Model (DK, normative SC, RWW, BOLD)
     - Run tvb (exported with tvbo) compare with tvboptim
   - Parameter-Sweep
   - Optimization (RWW workflow)
